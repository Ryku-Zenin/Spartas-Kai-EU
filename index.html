Spartas Kai was created on 10/20/2024 by Prozidole, Brambus12, and Diego_Box. Since then, it has been nothing but success. First place in the DOTM, multiple victories in All Valley, numerous wins in Mini-Tournaments, Cross Region, Double Podium, Triple Podium, and much more.

Prozidole and Brambus always made sure that players who hadn’t yet reached their first podium finally experienced that feeling of standing on it.

Here, we teach discipline, techniques, and adaptations so you can perform well regardless of your ping. We don’t seek competition. We create it.

Spartas Kai Rules

Keep it clear and strict. This prevents drama later.

  1. No toxicity. We want to provide a safe experience within our server, so please respect all server members and avoid drama.
  2. No discrimination of any kind. Racial slurs will result in a non-appealable ban. Bypassing racial slurs by censoring them or sending images/videos of them will still result in punishment.
  3. Do not argue. This includes arguing about a moderation decision.
  4. Do not attempt to scam anyone, out of anything.
  5. No evading punishments.
  6. Do not forge any evidence.
  7. No discussing controversial/sensitive topics (politics/religion)
  8. Do not post or discuss anything containing age-restricted (18+) content. This includes pornographic images/videos/links, gore, 18+ pfps/banners/names, etc.
  9. Do not harass members. Any form of harassment such as hate speech, blackmailing, doxxing, inappropriate DMs, impersonation, etc. will result in moderation action.
  10. Do not use banned words and/or bypassing banned words. This includes having banned words on your profile.
  11. No advertising any content (ex. YouTube videos/channels, discord servers, roblox groups, etc.) without permission. This includes DMs.
  12. Do not spam ping/annoy members.
  13. Do not spam or flood any chat for any reason. This includes giant text walls and copypastas.
  14. Do not send any ip grabbers or otherwise malicious links.
  15. You are required to be 13+ to use this discord server or any discord server. If you are found to be under the age of 13 you will be banned.
  16. Do not use alternate accounts to evade a punishment.
  17. Do not give a banned player alternate accounts, or knowingly lie to protect an alt.
